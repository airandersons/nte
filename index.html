Welcome to NTEC

We're a Bible-believing community in Kampala committed to making disciples who love God and love others.

Our Mission & Vision

To glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ through gospel-centered worship, teaching, fellowship, and outreach in our Kitintale community and beyond.

We believe the Bible is God's authoritative Word and the foundation for all we do. Under Bishop Peter Mutebi's leadership, our desire is to see lives transformed by the power of the Gospel.
