Welcome to NTEC

We're a Bible-believing community committed to making disciples who love God and love others.

Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ through gospel-centered worship, teaching, fellowship, and outreach.

We believe the Bible is God's authoritative Word and the foundation for all we do. Our desire is to see lives transformed by the power of the Gospel.
